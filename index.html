Harmonic Linguistics' Mission

Harmonic Linguistics aims to provide engaging products that help people explore and discover the rich genre of Hip Hop. As a way to thank the community that has produced such an amazing culture, eventually I'd like to accept donations which would go directly to supporting African American students in STEM and Creative Arts programs.
